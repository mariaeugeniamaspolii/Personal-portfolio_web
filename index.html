Fiat App

The project involved creating an interactive mobile digital prototype for use in the retro exhibition on the Fiat 125 car and other popular vehicles in Uruguay from the 60s to the 90s.

The prototype highlights the three most iconic models of the brand, providing details about their technical specifications and history.

Additionally, it presents an interactive timeline showcasing the evolution of Fiat car models over the years. Each point on the timeline represents a specific year, accompanied by a description of the corresponding model, giving visitors a comprehensive view of Fiat's progress over time.
